Getting Back to Full Employment:
A Better Bargain for Working People

Jared Bernstein and Dean Baker release the follow-up to their book, The Benefits of Full Employment (Economic Policy Institute, 2003). Getting Back to Full Employment builds on the evidence presented in their previous book, showing that real wage growth for workers in the bottom half of the income scale is highly dependent on the overall rate of unemployment.
